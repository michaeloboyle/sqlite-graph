Frequently Asked Questions

How does sqlite-graph compare to Neo4j or ArangoDB?

sqlite-graph is 500x smaller and 3000x faster to start. Perfect for embedded use cases, serverless functions, and applications that don't need a separate database server. For multi-TB graphs with complex analytics, consider Neo4j.

Can I migrate from self-hosted to cloud?

Yes! Export your data using db.export() and import it to sqlite-graph Cloud. Takes less than 5 minutes for most databases.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage. Operations continue with a small overage fee ($0.10 per 100K operations, $0.50 per GB storage). You can upgrade anytime.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR ready. Enterprise plans include dedicated encryption keys.

Can I use my existing SQLite database?

Yes! sqlite-graph is fully SQLite compatible. Upload your existing .db file and add graph capabilities on top. Your existing queries continue to work.

What's your uptime guarantee?

Starter: 99.9% (43 minutes/month downtime)
Pro: 99.99% (4 minutes/month)
Enterprise: Custom SLA up to 99.999%
